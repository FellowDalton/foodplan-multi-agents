# One-Pot Svinemørbrad Stew (Gryderet)

**Gluten-Free | Family of 4 | Cook Saturday, Reheat Sunday**

## Ingredients
- 2x svinemørbrad (pork tenderloin)
- 1.5 kg kartofler (potatoes)
- 3 porrer (leeks)
- 1 bunch gulerødder (carrots)
- 2-3 bullion cubes (chicken or vegetable)
- 700-800ml water
- 1 container fraiche 9%
- Optional: cocktailtomater, broccoli
- Seasonings: salt, pepper
- Butter or oil for browning

---

## Instructions (Saturday - 1.5 hours)

### Prep (15 minutes)
1. Cut svinemørbrad into 2-3cm cubes
2. Chop porrer into rings
3. Peel and cut gulerødder into chunks
4. Cut kartofler into bite-sized chunks
5. If using: halve cocktailtomater, break broccoli into florets

### Cooking (1 hour 15 minutes)
1. **Brown the pork** (5 minutes)
   - Heat butter/oil in large pot over medium-high heat
   - Add pork cubes in batches, brown on all sides
   - Remove and set aside

2. **Sauté vegetables** (5 minutes)
   - In same pot, add porrer and gulerødder
   - Cook until softened

3. **Simmer** (45-60 minutes)
   - Return pork to pot
   - Add kartofler chunks
   - Add 700-800ml water with 2-3 bullion cubes dissolved
   - Season with salt and pepper
   - Bring to boil, then reduce to simmer
   - Cover and cook 45-60 minutes until pork is tender and potatoes are soft

4. **Finish** (5 minutes)
   - Stir in fraiche/crème fraîche
   - If using broccoli, add florets and cook 5 minutes more
   - Taste and adjust seasoning
   - Optional: add cocktailtomater at the end for color

5. **Store**
   - Let cool to room temperature
   - Store in fridge in the pot (covered)

---

## Sunday - Reheating (20 minutes)

1. Place pot on stove over medium heat
2. Reheat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally
3. Add splash of water if too thick
4. Ensure heated through completely
5. Serve!

---

## Serving Suggestions
- Serve as-is (hearty stew/soup style)
- Serve over rice (using rice from home)
- Serve with GF bread on the side

---

✅ **Kid-friendly** - creamy, mild flavors, familiar vegetables
✅ **Reheats perfectly** - stews taste even better on day 2!
✅ **Hearty & filling** - satisfies everyone
✅ **Flexible** - skip vegetables kids don't like
✅ **Budget-friendly** - ~138 kr for 2 family dinners

---

## Tips
- Cut all vegetables roughly the same size for even cooking
- Don't skip browning the pork - it adds flavor!
- Taste before serving and adjust seasoning
- Kids don't like certain veggies? Leave them out or serve on the side
- Leftovers freeze well for up to 3 months
